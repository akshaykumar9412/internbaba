About ABC University

Established in 1965, ABC University has been a beacon of academic excellence for over 55 years. Our campus spans 200 acres and features state-of-the-art facilities including modern laboratories, extensive libraries, and recreational centers.

With over 15,000 students and 800 faculty members, we offer a vibrant and inclusive community that fosters innovation, creativity, and leadership.
